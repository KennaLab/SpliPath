Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Which genome build are the data based on?
A1: All the data are based on hg38/GRCh38.

Q2: What is "annotated junction" and "novel junction"?
A2: We combined ensembl GRCh38.98 annotation with the annotations described in Wilks et al., 2018. The "annotated" or "novel" means whether the junction exists in the annotation source. The coordinates of the junctions are 0-based.

Q3: Can I use another annotation source?
A3: Yes, it is possible to annotate junctions with other GTF annotation files using SpliPath R pacakge.
